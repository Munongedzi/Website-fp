Why we are here?

Most Financial Institutions struggle to profitably connect and manage their new & old clients’ risk and value. We provide with an Artificial Intelligence-based platform that allows them to deeply engage, retain and grow their customers and revenue with less risk.

On the other hand, there is a genuine lack of financial services for a large proportion of Zimbabwe’s poor and low-income individuals, as well as small and informal businesses that do not fit the mainstream financial profile. A large proportion of people and small businesses struggle to get access to valuable financial service so we created a convenient and hustle free platform that allows every individual to create wealth and live sustainably the way they want.
