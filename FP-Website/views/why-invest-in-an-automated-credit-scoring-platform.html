Why Invest in an Automated Credit Scoring Platform

1. Operational efficiency gains

a. Reduce customer turnaround with fewer in-person interactions
b. Lower administrative costs per unit
c. Lower the number of in-person interactions with prospective borrowed

2. Improved accuracy of credit decisions (targeted lending based on default probability)

a. Minimize rejection of creditworthy applicants
b. Maximize rejection of high-risk applicants

3. Establishment of an objective and standardized data-driven decision-making culture

a. Apply base objective and consistent decision making on empirical evidence from different risk factors and data points.
b. Standardize criteria for decision making
c. Minimize room for human error or bias

4. Loan uptake effectiveness

a. Target loan products to increase conversion rate

5. Customer satisfaction improvement (faster, more targeted loans)

a. Reduce customer turnaround
b. Pre-approve specific products, upgrades, and cross-sells
c. Low customer acquisition costs

6. Enhance Customer Relationships and Experience

a. Reduction in customer attrition rates
b. Assign credit limits based on risk level
c. Price loan products based on risk level
d. Adjust terms and shorten repayment schedules
e. Monitor the customer repayment process through risk alerts from the system
f. Offer additional products and upgrades
g. Offer automated renewals for specific products
h. Prequalify customers for new products or cross-sell products
i. Quantify present and future customer lifetime value
