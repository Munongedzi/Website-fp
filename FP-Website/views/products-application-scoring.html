Application Scoring

Lenders may not be sure which clients from those who applied for a loan are most likely to default a loan in the future. Application scoring models classify applicants according to their risk profiles which enables accurate decisions and better pricing. The classification will be done from different sources of data. The application scoring models will be based on; a) historical client information, b) psychometric personality results, c) text written by applicants during application and lastly social media data.
